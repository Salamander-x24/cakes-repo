
Ingredients
For the cake;

4 eggs (whites and yolks are seperated),
1/2 cup of sugar,
1 cup of flour,
10 g of vanilin sugar.
For syrup;

3 cups of milk,
1/2 cup of sugar,
10 g of vanilin sugar.
For pudding;

4 cups of milk,
2 heaping spoon of flour,
2 heaping spoon of starch,
1/2 cup of sugar,
1 egg,
1 tbsp of butter,
10 g of vanilin sugar.
For topping;

Grated coconut.
